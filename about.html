About Us 🪄

Rajashree CakeStory is a home-based bakery built with passion, creativity, and love. What started as a small interest in baking has now grown into a beautiful journey of spreading happiness through cakes.

Each cake is freshly made using quality ingredients and designed with attention to every little detail. Whether it’s a birthday, anniversary, or a simple celebration, every creation is made to make your moments special and memorable.

With the constant support of loved ones and the trust of wonderful customers, this journey continues to grow beautifully. Every order motivates us to keep creating and spreading happiness — one cake at a time ❤️
